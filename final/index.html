Masala chai is an extremely popular South Asian spiced black tea. In many South Asian languages, "Masala" means "spice," and "chai" means tea. The popularity of the drink is not constricted to South Asia; over the years, "chai" has become a global craze, available and popular at most coffee shops in Western countries.
